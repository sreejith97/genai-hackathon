# Let me explain what I built.
I built an app called SpeakUp to help people who feel like their doctors are not truly listening to them. You just open it in your web browser without needing to sign up, and you can either type, use your voice, or even upload a photo of your medical reports to explain what happened. A smart computer program then reads your situation and gives you helpful tools: it tells you honestly if your symptoms deserved more attention, organizes your history into a neat timeline, gives you a list of specific questions to ask your doctor next time, and writes a polite letter that you can send to the clinic if you feel you were not treated properly. It is important to know that this is not a real doctor and cannot diagnose you or give you medicine, but is instead like a well-read friend who helps you advocate for yourself. I made this because many people get intimidated at the doctor's office, and this gives them a little bit of courage and the exact words they need to feel heard.
